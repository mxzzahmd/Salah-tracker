A little side project I made because mainstream trackers are so cluttered and have way too many advertisements.

open the url https://mxzzahmd.github.io/Salah-tracker/ to see the tracker, and share to homescreen to get it on your phone screen

_uses **LOCALDATA**, meaning all data is stored on **YOUR** device, and will not transfer between devices_


##  How to Use the App

### 1. Initial Setup (First Time Only)
1. Open the app on your phone's browser and use the **Share** menu to select **"Add to Home Screen"** to install it as a standalone app.
2. Open the installed app from your home screen.
3. Scroll to the top section, type in your **City** and **Country**, and tap **"Set Location & Times"**. This automatically pulls your local daily prayer timings.
4. Tap the green **"🔔 Enable Dynamic Notifications"** button and grant permission when prompted by your phone to activate automatic alerts.

---

### 2. Tracking Your Prayers
* The main screen displays the 5 daily prayers along with their calculated times.
* When you complete a prayer, simply **tap the button**. It will turn **dark green** to show it is logged.
* If you made a mistake, tap it again to uncheck it. 
* Your selections are saved automatically and will remain locked in even if you close the app.

---

### 3. Logging Your Quran Recitation
* Scroll down to the **Quran Recitation** section.
* Use the dropdown menu to select the specific **Surah** you are reading.
* Enter your starting verse in the **"From Verse"** box, and your ending verse in the **"To Verse"** box.
* Tap **"Save Progress"**. 
* Your entry will immediately appear at the top of the **Recitation History** list below, stamped with the exact date and time you completed it.
